------------------------------------
Modular Lowpoly Track Free
------------------------------------

	Modular Lowpoly Track Free contains 14 meshes and 14 prefabs with 1 track.

	Features:

		• 7 corners modules.
		• 3 fences modules.
		• 2 straight modules of the road.
		• 1 start/finish.
		• 1 lawn.
		• One texture 64×64 for all meshes.

	Compatible:

		• Unity 2018.4.0f1 or higher.

	Thanks for reviews :)
